Indian architecture

Architectural Traditions of india

The Indian architecture soaked in history.culture and tradition. Most of the grand monument have built by the rulers who governed us.They show their power and authority in that way.Medieval indian architecture shows a rich diversity.They display a fusion of existing style and new ideas brought by invaders.

Historical monuments are very old buildings which have existed many centuries.They can be divided into 2 categories --- Religious building and secular biuldingsreligious Buildings - Temples,mosques,churches,stupas and other places for worship. Secular buildings - Forts, places, tombs, halls, havelis and cities.

Hindu Temple Architecture

Two distinct style of temple building emerged in india.They are the nagara or the north Indian style and the Dravida or the south Indian style.In both indian style stone pillars and walls are used to support the roof.
