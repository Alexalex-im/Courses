Types of Nutrient

There are two types of nutrient seen among the living organisms -- autotrophic, hetrotrophic nutrient

Autotrophic Nutrient

When an organism makes it own food from simple inorganic substances then this is called autotrophic nutrient and such organisms are called autotrops

Hetrotrophic Nutrient

The process of taking food from green plants directly or indirectly is called hetrotrophic nutrient and such organisms are called hetrotrops
